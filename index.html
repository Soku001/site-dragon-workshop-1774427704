About Dragon Workshop

Your trusted jeweler in the heart of Clifton, Bristol.

At Dragon Workshop, located at 47 Princess Victoria St, Clifton, Bristol, we pride ourselves on being a premium jeweler dedicated to timeless elegance and exceptional service. With a passion for fine craftsmanship, our Expert Team brings decades of experience to every piece, ensuring Quality Guaranteed.

We believe that every piece of jewelry tells a story. From bespoke engagement rings to stunning anniversary gifts, we offer a Free Consultation to help you find or create the perfect expression of your unique style and sentiment. Our commitment to excellence and personalized Customer Support sets us apart, making your experience with us as precious as the jewels we offer.
